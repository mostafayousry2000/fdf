FEBRUARY 12, 2020

New Album: Sahran Released

Amr Diab released his new album (Sahran) in the last minutes of the 12th of February 2020. Listen to new album exclusively on Vodafone Egypt, Dial *22# from your Vodafone line to get the new album.

The new album consists of 12 new tracks: Zay Manty, Aam El Tabeeb, Ya Rawa’anek, Hayeish Yeftekerni, Bel Dehka Di, Makanak Fe Alby, Rooh, Gamila, Mahragan, Sahran and Gamda Bas. In addition to 2019’s hits: Bahebo, Odam Merayetha, Youm Talat and Awel Youm Fi Elboad. Sahran Album is produced by Nay for Media.

JULY 30, 2020

Ya Baladna Ya Helwa; New Single Released

Amr Diab released his latest track “Ya Baladna Ya Helwa” through Vodafone Music. Ya Baladna Ya Helwa music video showed how beautiful Egyptians and Egypt cities are.

Music video has been shot in various locations in Egypt under the direction of: Tarek El Eryan. Song lyrics by: Tamer Hussien, Composed by: Aziz El Shafei and Music arrangement by: Tarek Madkour.
